About

mtsg (Mike the Science Guy) is a Salida-based DJ and producer blending electronic, trap, hip hop, and bass into dynamic open-format sets. Inspired by artists like Flume and MEMBA, mtsg is known for live edits and one-of-a-kind performances, treating the mixing board like an instrument to create spontaneous blends where rhythm becomes the universal language.
